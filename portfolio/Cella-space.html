Client Brief:

Establishing a brand takes up a huge chunk of work, but to rebrand an existing organization has its own challenges. Cellaspace approached us for the sole purpose of rebranding and to give a new face to their revamped business approach.

Solution:

Any rebranding activity starts with the change of logo. For Cellaspace we began with the redesign of the logo, we came up with brand new logo that resonated with the company. We completely rebranded and redesigned all BTL products like business cards, stall design and brochures. Additionally, we designed a brand new website for Cellaspace as a part of the rebranding strategy.

Impact:

Our solutions created huge positive impact for the client. The client sold around 85% of the initial 12000 sq. ft space for lease. This proven sales and its generated profits had led them to expand to other areas and also increase infrastructure spaces.
