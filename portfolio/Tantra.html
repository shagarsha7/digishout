Client Brief:

In an increasingly digitalized world, wireless connectivity is an essential requirement. The need of the hour is proper seamless connectivity with devices in any part of the building you are in. Tantra was a new entrant into the Indian market for cloud WIFI services catering to homes and SMB segment.

Solution:

For Tantra we conceptualized, designed and printed out a high-quality brochure for the Indian market. The brochure design took the existing brand design language into consideration while creating the new design.

Impact:

The client was impressed with the high-quality work received. The brochure by itself had an instant positive impact among the public, which took the word out for the brand. This design solution brought forth new leads for the client.
